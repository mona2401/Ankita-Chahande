About US

Hi! I'm Ankita Chahande.

Ankita always wanted to bring about a change in everyone's perception of mental health, and so after graduating as an Engineer, she chose to become a Psychotherapist. Ankita's favorite part about her job is being witness to the transformation that her clients go through once they start therapy. She believes that it's mostly like the 'Butterfly Effect'- when one person goes through a significant positive change, it impacts 4 more people related to them. Ankita gets excited by the idea of creating a change, no matter how small or big it may seem. For her, it could even be cooking the most regular meal in a different way. A lover of metaphysics, she enjoys talking to like-minded people. Ankita is a certified Career counselor by MoE&L. Assertive communication style is one of her go-to ways for counselling. She is an explorer. She hopes to travel the world, and by that, she not only means going to famous landmarks and locations, but also the less talked about cities. As much as Ankita loves everything around her, she loves herself the most.
